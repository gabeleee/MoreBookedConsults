Plastic surgery CRO isn't med spa CRO with different photos. The economics are different: higher average case value, decision cycles of three to twelve months, and a gallery-and-financing decision most med spas never face.

## <span className="he">📈</span> Where the consult funnel leaks

Visitors arrive ready to research, not book. The leaks are specific:

- **Inquiry forms that ask too much:** long forms lose high-intent patients mid-way
- **Procedure pages with no pricing or financing signal:** patients need a sense of investment before they'll request a consult
- **Before/after galleries buried or gated:** your strongest proof, hidden
- **No clear handoff to a coordinator:** the consult never gets booked

## <span className="he">🎯</span> What's different about testing for surgery

Longer cycles mean attribution is harder and traffic minimums matter more.

A low-traffic surgical site can't support the same test cadence as a busy med spa. We'll tell you that honestly in the audit rather than run tests that can't reach significance.

</Callout>
