Plastic surgery CRO isn't medspa CRO with different photos. The economics are different: higher average case value, decision cycles of three to twelve months, and a gallery-and-financing decision most med spas never face.

## Where the consult funnel leaks

Visitors arrive ready to research, not book. The leaks are specific:

- **Inquiry forms that ask too much**, long forms lose high-intent patients mid-way
- **Procedure pages with no pricing or financing signal**, patients need a sense of investment before they'll request a consult
- **Before/after galleries buried or gated**, your strongest proof, hidden
- **No clear handoff to a coordinator**, the consult never gets booked

## What's different about testing for surgery

Longer cycles mean attribution is harder and traffic minimums matter more, a low-traffic surgical site can't support the same test cadence as a busy med spa, and we'll tell you that honestly during the audit. What gets tested first: inquiry forms, gallery UX, financing placement, and consult CTA framing.
