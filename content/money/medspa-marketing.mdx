Most medspa marketing advice starts with channels, run more ads, post more Reels. That's backwards. Start with the math every owner already knows in their gut.

## <span className="he">🎯</span> The owner's mental model

Booked consults come from a simple equation:

**Booked consults = traffic × conversion rate × show rate.**

You can buy more traffic, but it's the most expensive lever and the one you least control. The two levers you *do* control, and the two this practice runs, are conversion and local ranking.

<Callout type="stat">

At LaserAway, founder Gabe Meierotto grew sitewide conversion from **3% to 11%**, same traffic, more than 3× the bookings. Those are his personal results in that role, not a promise for your practice.

</Callout>

## <span className="he">📈</span> Lever one: convert the traffic you already have

Your booking flow, intro offers, treatment pages, and hero forms are where consults leak. Fixing them costs nothing in ad spend and compounds every month afterward. The usual leaks:

- Slow, clunky mobile booking
- Intro offers buried three scrolls down
- Treatment pages that read like a brochure
- Thin social proof where the decision happens

We run [one controlled test a month](/medspa-cro/) on the highest-impact spot, long enough to trust the result. If you want the benchmark first, start with [what a good med spa conversion rate looks like](/blog/medspa-conversion-rate-benchmarks/).

## <span className="he">🔍</span> Lever two: rank for the treatments your city searches

When someone nearby searches "lip filler" or "laser hair removal" plus your city, your practice should be the answer. That's the [local SEO](/medspa-seo/) half:

- A dialed-in Google Business Profile
- Steady, recent reviews
- Consistent citations
- Treatment-by-city content that matches real searches

Full walkthrough: [how to rank a med spa on Google Maps](/blog/how-to-rank-medspa-google-maps/).
