Most medspa marketing advice starts with channels — run more ads, post more Reels. That's backwards. Start with the math every owner already knows in their gut:

**Booked consults = traffic × conversion rate × show rate.**

You can buy more traffic, but it's the most expensive lever and the one you least control. The two levers you *do* control — and the two this practice runs — are conversion and local ranking.

## Lever one: convert the traffic you already have

Your booking flow, intro offers, treatment pages, and hero forms are where consults leak. Fixing them costs nothing in ad spend and compounds every month afterward.

We run [one controlled test a month](/medspa-cro/) on the highest-impact spot — long enough to trust the result, so wins stack instead of canceling out. If you want the benchmark first, start with [what a good med spa conversion rate looks like](/blog/medspa-conversion-rate-benchmarks/).

## Lever two: rank for the treatments your city searches

When someone nearby searches "lip filler" or "laser hair removal" plus your city, your practice should be the answer. That's [treatment-by-city content, a clean Google Business Profile, citations, and review velocity](/medspa-seo/) — the local SEO half.

## How the work actually runs

Every engagement starts with a free audit of your site and rankings, ranked by revenue impact. From there: a prioritized plan, one controlled test a month, and a plain-English report on what it did to your bookings.
