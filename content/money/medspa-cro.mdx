Most med spa sites convert a low single-digit percentage of visitors into booked consults. The difference between 3% and 6% isn't more ad spend — it's a booking flow that doesn't leak. Before anything else, know [what a good med spa conversion rate actually is](/blog/medspa-conversion-rate-benchmarks/).

## One controlled test a month

The whole model is one clean, controlled test per month — run long enough to reach significance, so you learn what actually moved bookings instead of guessing. Twelve valid tests a year compound; a dozen half-finished experiments don't.

## What gets tested first

We start where the money leaks, ranked by revenue impact from your free audit:

- **Booking forms** — [multi-step forms consistently outperform](/blog/medspa-multi-step-forms/) single long ones
- **Intro-offer placement and framing** — the offer that converts first-time patients
- **Treatment pages** — answering "is this right for me, and what does it cost?"
- **Hero and above-the-fold** — the first five seconds decide most sessions

## What you get

A prioritized plan, one test built and run each month, and a plain-English report: what we tested, what it did to bookings, and what's next.
