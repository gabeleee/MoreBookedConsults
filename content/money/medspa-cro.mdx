Most med spa sites convert a low single-digit percentage of visitors into booked consults. The difference between 3% and 6% isn't more ad spend. It's a booking flow that doesn't leak.

## <span className="he">📈</span> One controlled test a month

The whole model is one clean, controlled test per month, run long enough to reach significance. You learn what actually moved bookings instead of guessing.

<Callout type="key">

Twelve valid tests a year compound. A dozen half-finished experiments don't. The discipline is the product.

</Callout>

## <span className="he">🎯</span> What gets tested first

We start where the money leaks, ranked by revenue impact from your free audit:

- **Booking forms:** [multi-step forms consistently outperform](/blog/medspa-multi-step-forms/) single long ones
- **Intro-offer placement and framing:** the offer that converts first-time patients
- **Treatment pages:** answering "is this right for me, and what does it cost?"
- **Hero and above-the-fold:** the first five seconds decide most sessions

1. A prioritized plan from the free audit
