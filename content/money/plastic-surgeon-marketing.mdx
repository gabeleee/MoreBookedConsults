The owner's mental model for a surgical practice is a funnel, not a megaphone:

**Cases = inquiries × consult rate × close rate × average case value.**

At surgical case values, a single extra converted consult a month can exceed an entire marketing retainer. That's why the two levers here are conversion and ranking — not more top-of-funnel spend.

## Lever one: convert inquiries into booked consultations

Consult-request forms, procedure pages, financing clarity, and before/after galleries are where high-value consults leak. We run [one controlled test a month](/plastic-surgery-cro/) on the spot that matters most — run to significance, so you know what actually worked.

## Lever two: rank for the procedures your market searches

A patient searching "rhinoplasty" or "breast augmentation" plus your city should find your practice first. That's the [local ranking + procedure-page authority](/plastic-surgery-seo/) layer.
