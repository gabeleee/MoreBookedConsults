Every med spa owner who's been burned by advertising has the same story: the ads ran, the clicks came, the bookings didn't.

The usual diagnosis, "the ads were bad," is usually wrong. The ads sent people to a site that leaked them, and no amount of ad tuning fixes a leaky page.

## <span className="he">🪣</span> The leak is the real problem

<Callout type="key">

At a typical booking rate, most of your ad spend funds visitors who leave without booking. You're paying for the 97 percent who bounce.

Fixing conversion means every one of those same dollars works harder, which is why [CRO](/medspa-cro/) is the highest-return advertising decision you can make, even though it isn't advertising at all.

</Callout>

Double your conversion rate and you roughly halve your cost per booked consult, without touching your ad budget. That's the leverage owners overlook while they keep tweaking headlines.
