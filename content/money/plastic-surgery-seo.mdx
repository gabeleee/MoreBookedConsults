Ranking a plastic surgery practice is a longer game than a med spa, but the mechanics are the same two layers, applied to surgery.

## <span className="he">🔍</span> The two layers

- **The local pack:** where the practitioner-versus-practice Google Business Profile question bites. Multi-surgeon practices have to decide how listings map to individual surgeons versus the practice. Getting this wrong splits your ranking signal.
- **Procedure-page authority:** your patient-facing procedure pages, structured so Google trusts them as the authority for "rhinoplasty" or "breast augmentation" in your market.

## <span className="he">⭐</span> Reviews and RealSelf

For surgical practices, reviews and RealSelf presence carry real weight in the decision and in rankings. A steady review cadence is part of the retainer, not an afterthought.
