Most plastic surgery marketing agencies sell traffic: more ads, more impressions, a bigger top of funnel.

But at surgical case values, the expensive leak isn't awareness.

It's the visitor who researched your practice, compared your before/afters, almost inquired, and left.

Fix that, and the traffic you already have is worth far more, which is why this practice starts with the funnel rather than the ad account.

Booked consultations. Not reach, and not rankings for their own sake, but consultations on the schedule.

Everything this practice does ladders up to that one number, through two services:

- **[Conversion optimization](/plastic-surgery-cro/)** on the consult funnel: inquiry forms, procedure pages, financing clarity, gallery UX.
- **[Local SEO](/plastic-surgery-seo/):** the map pack plus procedure-page authority in your market.

## <span className="he">📉</span> Where surgical practices actually leak

The funnel runs visitor to inquiry to consultation to surgery, and the money hides in the middle.

Website conversion is the cheapest stage to fix: no extra ad spend, and it compounds.

At these case values the math is stark, since one extra converted consult a month can exceed the entire cost of the work.
