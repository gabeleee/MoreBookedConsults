## <span className="he">🔍</span> The two layers

A med spa's local SEO lives in two places, and both matter.

- **The local map pack.** A dialed-in Google Business Profile, accurate citations, and steady, recent review velocity, so you show up in the map results when someone nearby searches. Google ranks the pack on relevance, distance, and prominence, and reviews are the strongest prominence signal you actually control.
- **Treatment-by-city authority.** Pages that genuinely answer the treatment questions patients ask, in the market you serve, built into a topical structure Google trusts, so you also rank in the organic results below the map.

The full walkthrough is [how to rank a med spa on Google Maps](/blog/how-to-rank-medspa-google-maps/).

## <span className="he">⭐</span> Reviews do the heavy lifting

Of everything in local SEO, reviews move the needle hardest.

Count, velocity, and recency are among the strongest prominence signals, and they're also what convinces the human reading the map pack to click you over the practice next door.

<Callout type="key">

A steady review habit beats a one-time push every time.

Respond to every review, and keep the replies HIPAA-safe: never confirm that someone is a patient.

</Callout>
