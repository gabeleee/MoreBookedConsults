<AtAGlance>

- **Three levers grow a med spa:** get found, convert, retain. Most owners only chase the first.
- **Cheapest lever:** convert the traffic you already have.
- **Most valuable lever:** retention. A repeat patient beats a new one every time.
- **Offers acquire; memberships retain.** Use both deliberately.

</AtAGlance>

"More clients" feels like one problem, but it's really three, and owners almost always over-invest in the most expensive one. Here's the full picture.

## <span className="he">🎯</span> The three levers

Every new booking comes from one of three places:

- **Get found:** more of the right people discover you ([local SEO](/medspa-seo/), ads, social, referrals).
- **Convert:** more of the people who already find you actually book ([CRO](/medspa-cro/)).
- **Retain:** the patients you have come back and bring others.

Buying traffic is the most expensive and least controllable. Start with converting and retaining, then scale traffic once the funnel holds water.

## <span className="he">📣</span> Getting found

The channels, ranked by durability for most med spas:

- **Local SEO and the map pack:** compounding, and [covered in its own guide](/blog/how-to-rank-medspa-google-maps/)
- **Referrals:** your cheapest, highest-trust source, see [referral programs](/blog/medspa-referral-programs/)
- **Paid ads:** fast but rented, see [Google Ads](/blog/medspa-google-ads/) and [Facebook and Instagram](/blog/medspa-facebook-instagram-ads/)
- **Social and events:** [Instagram](/blog/medspa-instagram-marketing/) and [open houses](/blog/medspa-events-open-houses/)

## <span className="he">💸</span> Offers that acquire the right patient

<Callout type="key">

An intro offer's job isn't to sell a cheap treatment. It's to acquire a patient who comes back. Structure it around the second visit, not the first.

</Callout>

## <span className="he">🔁</span> Retention: the lever owners ignore

The cheapest client to book is the one you already have. Retention runs on:

- [Membership programs](/blog/medspa-membership-programs/) that create recurring revenue
- [Email](/blog/medspa-email-marketing/) and [SMS](/blog/medspa-sms-marketing/) sequences that rebook
- A [client-retention and rebooking habit](/blog/medspa-client-retention/) at the front desk
