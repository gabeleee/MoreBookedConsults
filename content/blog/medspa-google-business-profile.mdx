Your Google Business Profile is the most important thing you control in local search, and most med spas leave half of it empty.

It's the panel that shows up in the map pack and beside your name in search, and how complete and active it is directly affects whether you rank at all.

The good news: fixing it costs nothing but attention.

## <span className="he">🏷️</span> The primary category decides your rankings

Of every field on the profile, the primary category matters most.

It tells Google which searches you're even eligible to appear for, so "Medical spa" and "Skin care clinic" and "Weight loss service" are three different pools of visibility.

<Callout type="key">

Choose your primary category deliberately, usually "Medical spa," then add secondary categories for the specific services you offer.

Defaulting to a vague category is one of the most common and most costly profile mistakes.

</Callout>

## <span className="he">🧰</span> Services, attributes, and details

Once the category is right, fill the rest completely.

- **Services.** List the actual treatments you offer, using the names patients search.
- **Attributes.** Accessibility, amenities, and booking options, all of which can surface in results.
- **Hours.** Accurate, including holidays, because wrong hours erode trust and rankings.
- **Description and contact.** A clear description with your real phone and website.

Every blank field is a small signal you're leaving on the table.

## <span className="he">📸</span> Photos and posts

Real photos of your space, team, and results outperform stock, and profiles with strong photo sets get more engagement.

Posts are a free surface almost no med spa uses: a weekly update, offer, or treatment highlight signals an active business and keeps the profile fresh.

Neither takes much time, and both compound.

## <span className="he">🔗</span> The booking link

Add a direct booking or appointment link to the profile.

It shortens the path from a "med spa near me" search to a scheduled appointment, and Google surfaces it prominently, capturing high-intent searchers before they even reach your website.

For a busy profile, this single link can be a meaningful source of bookings on its own.

## <span className="he">⚠️</span> Common mistakes

- A vague or wrong primary category
- Duplicate profiles from an old location or rebrand, which split your signal
- Ignoring the [reviews](/blog/medspa-get-more-reviews/) and Q&A sections
- Never posting, so the profile looks dormant

The profile is step one of the [full local SEO stack](/blog/how-to-rank-medspa-google-maps/); get it airtight before anything else.
