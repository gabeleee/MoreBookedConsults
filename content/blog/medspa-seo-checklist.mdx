<AtAGlance>

- **Foundation first:** indexable, fast, mobile, one clear topic per page.
- **On-page:** a real title and H1, treatment-plus-city targeting, useful content.
- **Local:** complete Google Business Profile, consistent citations, schema.
- **Skip the advanced stuff** until the basics are airtight.

</AtAGlance>

Most med spa SEO problems aren't exotic. They're basics left undone: a slow site, a page accidentally hidden from Google, a homepage trying to rank for everything and therefore nothing.

This checklist is the fundamentals, in priority order, because doing the basics right beats doing advanced tactics on a broken foundation.

## <span className="he">🧱</span> The foundation (do these first)

If any of these is broken, nothing else matters.

- **Indexable.** Confirm your key pages aren't accidentally blocked or noindexed, a shockingly common launch mistake.
- **Fast.** [Speed and mobile performance](/blog/medspa-website-speed-mobile/) affect both rankings and conversion.
- **Mobile-friendly.** Google indexes the mobile version, and most of your patients are on phones anyway.
- **One clear topic per page.** A page that tries to cover everything ranks for nothing.

## <span className="he">📝</span> On-page basics

Once the foundation is solid, get the on-page elements right.

- **Title tag.** A clear, specific title per page, targeting its treatment-plus-city query.
- **One H1.** The page's main heading, matching what the page is about.
- **Useful content.** Genuinely answer the searcher's question, don't stuff keywords.
- **Internal links.** Link related pages into a [topical structure](/blog/medspa-keyword-research/) so Google understands your authority.

## <span className="he">📍</span> Local essentials

For a med spa, local signals do much of the heavy lifting.

- A complete, correctly-categorized [Google Business Profile](/blog/medspa-google-business-profile/)
- Consistent [citations](/blog/medspa-citations-directories/) across the important directories
- A steady [review](/blog/medspa-get-more-reviews/) habit
- **Local business schema** on your site, so search engines can read your name, address, and hours cleanly

## <span className="he">⚙️</span> Don't over-engineer it

<Callout type="key">

You don't need enterprise-level technical SEO to rank a med spa. You need the fundamentals done right.

Owners who chase advanced tactics while their site is slow and their profile is half-blank are optimizing the wrong end. Get the checklist above airtight first.

</Callout>
