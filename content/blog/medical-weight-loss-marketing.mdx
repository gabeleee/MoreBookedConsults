Medical weight loss, driven by GLP-1 medications like semaglutide, is one of the highest-demand services a med spa can offer right now.

It's also one of the trickiest to advertise, so the practices doing well are the ones that understand both the opportunity and the constraints.

## <span className="he">📈</span> Demand and seasonality

Demand here is strong year-round and unusually high compared to most aesthetic treatments, though it spikes with new-year resolutions and pre-summer motivation.

Unlike a seasonal body treatment, weight loss interest rarely goes quiet, which makes it a steady demand engine when you can reach patients compliantly.

The challenge isn't finding demand. It's connecting to it without tripping ad policy.

## <span className="he">💰</span> Offer economics: it's a program

<Callout type="key">

Weight loss is inherently ongoing, so price and market it as a program, not a per-injection transaction.

A monthly membership or program structure matches the clinical reality and creates recurring, predictable revenue, which makes GLP-1 one of the most valuable additions to a practice's economics.

</Callout>

Frame the price around the full journey, the medication, the monitoring, and the support, so patients understand they're buying a supervised result, not a syringe. That framing both converts better and reflects the [lifetime value](/blog/medspa-patient-lifetime-value/) of a program patient.

## <span className="he">⚖️</span> Ad-policy constraints (the hard part)

<Callout type="warning">

Weight loss and prescription-drug advertising is among the most restricted categories on Google and Meta, with limits on drug names, before-and-after imagery, health claims, and targeting.

Assume you cannot advertise this the way you'd advertise a facial, and build compliant creative from the start. Read the [healthcare ad policy node](/blog/medspa-healthcare-ad-policy/) before spending a dollar.

</Callout>

Because paid channels are so constrained, weight loss rewards owned channels: [local SEO](/medspa-seo/), educational content, and [referrals](/blog/medspa-referral-programs/) that reach demand without fighting ad platforms.

## <span className="he">🎯</span> The conversion angle

Weight loss patients want trust and safety far more than a discount.

Lead with medical credibility, supervision, and honest, non-guaranteed expectations, and make starting feel supported rather than transactional, because [your page's job](/medspa-cro/) here is to convert a serious, cautious decision into a confident first step.
