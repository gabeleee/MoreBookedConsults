Laser resurfacing sits at the premium, high-impact end of skin treatments, capable of dramatic results and requiring real recovery.

That combination makes honesty about downtime not just ethical but the core of what converts serious buyers.

## <span className="he">📈</span> Demand and seasonality

<Callout type="key">

Resurfacing involves meaningful downtime and heightened sun sensitivity, so fall and winter are the natural selling seasons.

Position it as the cooler-months skin renewal patients can recover from comfortably, which fits the timeline and fills demand when summer-driven treatments slow.

</Callout>

The counter-seasonal fit lets you promote resurfacing exactly when your body-treatment demand quiets, balancing the calendar.

## <span className="he">💰</span> Offer economics: premium and high-ticket

Ablative and fractional resurfacing command higher prices and often deliver strong results in fewer sessions than gentler treatments.

That makes it a considered, high-ticket purchase where the marketing leans on results and expertise, not discounting, and each converted patient represents significant revenue, sometimes with financing options aiding the decision.

## <span className="he">⚖️</span> Ad-policy constraints

Laser resurfacing is a device treatment without injectable or prescription-drug restrictions, so paid and social have room to work.

Keep results claims honest, and use realistic [before-and-afters with consent](/blog/medspa-before-after-photos/), which are persuasive for resurfacing precisely because the results can be striking when shown credibly.

## <span className="he">🎯</span> The conversion angle

<Callout type="warning">

Downtime honesty is everything. Patients who aren't prepared for recovery feel misled and review harshly.

</Callout>

Lead with the dramatic, results-focused promise, then be transparent about recovery, and let your [page](/medspa-cro/) treat the patient as a serious buyer making a considered choice, because pairing striking results with honest recovery is what converts the premium resurfacing patient.
