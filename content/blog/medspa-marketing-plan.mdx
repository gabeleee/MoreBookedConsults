<AtAGlance>

- **Foundations before spend.** Conversion, profile, tracking, reviews first.
- **Month 1-2 build,** month 3-4 turn on demand, month 5-6 optimize and retain.
- **Each phase compounds** on the one before it.
- **Sequence beats budget** in the early months.

</AtAGlance>

A marketing plan isn't a list of tactics, it's an order of operations, and getting the order right is what separates practices that build momentum from ones that spin.

Here's a six-month roadmap that front-loads the foundations, so every later dollar works harder.

## <span className="he">🧱</span> Months 1-2: build the foundation

<Callout type="key">

Before you spend a dollar on ads, get the fundamentals right, because they multiply everything that follows.

A site that [converts](/medspa-cro/), a complete Google Business Profile, a steady review habit, and clean [tracking](/blog/medspa-attribution-call-tracking/) are the foundation. Skip them and you're pouring water into a leaky bucket.

</Callout>

This phase feels slow because it isn't flashy, but it's the highest-leverage work you'll do all year.

## <span className="he">📣</span> Months 3-4: turn on demand

With the foundation solid, start driving traffic that now has somewhere good to land.

- **[Local SEO](/medspa-seo/)** to build the owned channel that compounds
- **Targeted [ads](/blog/medspa-google-ads/)** in the channels that fit your treatments
- **[Referrals](/blog/medspa-referral-programs/)** built into every happy visit

Because your site converts and your tracking works, you can see what's paying off instead of guessing.

## <span className="he">📈</span> Months 5-6: optimize and retain

Now you refine, and you make patients stick.

Double down on the channels your data shows are working, cut what isn't, and shift focus to [retention](/blog/medspa-client-retention/): [memberships](/blog/medspa-membership-programs/), a rebooking habit, and win-back sequences that turn first visits into lasting revenue.

Retention is what makes month six worth more than month one, because you're no longer starting from zero each month.

## <span className="he">🔁</span> Why the sequence matters

<Callout type="note">

Each phase compounds on the last. Foundations make demand-gen efficient; demand-gen fills the funnel that retention then keeps full.

</Callout>

Practices that skip to ad spend before fixing conversion waste most of the budget, so let [what you spend](/blog/medspa-marketing-budget/) follow the roadmap, not race ahead of it.
